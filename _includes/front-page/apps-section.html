The AWAKENING : Shared Vibrations

An open-source platform to share Image, Audio and Video with devices synchronised to the millisecond. A 'Session Creator' to control the collective playback and final selection of content for a playlist. A simple idea, create shared playlists between people within proximity with all devices playing in concert.

However the idea quicky grew into a platfom we hope to be used globally for Media Producer Broadcasting, Synchronised Meditations, Yoga and Sound Therapy.

The basic premise: Sound is the first sense we develop as infants and is the most powerful sense we have to illicit emotions. Using basic principles in physics such as harmonics, constructive wave formations, Binareal Beats (perceived sound diferentials) and the fact that the interaction of individuals when combined produce a total effect that is greater than the sum of any individual contributor (synergism) we can create a stronger manifestation of these emotions with participants across the globe. Very Hippy, we know, but we have high hopes for this as a medium for Global Healing.

P2P Banking : Fee-Free Money

A new Transactions Platform based on a derivative of the Blockchain technology made infamous by Bitcoin, the first truly global shared ledger.

The API allows transactions to flow between parties within 1-2 seconds to facilitate POS transactions instead of longer clearance times with current cryptocurrencies.

Not a Cryptocurrency. It can be used with any asset-class or crypto-contract, but a technology to facilitate transaction validation using Multi-party Cyptographic Protocols between 'trusted parties' instead of CPU work across the network.

Personal Banking. Any Asset

Designed for personal banking, POS transactions and investment. An ability to transact using any asset class - Currencies, Derivatives, Comodities, Debt Instruments, Micro-Investments, micro-loans...etc.
