BERRY-BERRY : Learn English Online

It is generally accepted that English today is arguably the most important language for people to learn. Unfortunately, many non-native speakers around the world have very limited access to well-structured lessons taught by native English teachers.

Hence we created Berry Berry; a fun and affordable new online platform where anyone can come to receive easy to follow and progressive game-based English lessons where newly acquired skills can be practiced with native English speakers.

By teaming up with qualified English teachers with more than 50 years of shared expertise, and integrative software that allows for voice recognition assessment, conversation transcripts and quantitative reporting among many other features we have developed a comprehensive EAL programme that will ensure that instructors have the support they need to provide meaningful and free-flowing conversations.

Shared VIBRATIONS : Link'n'Sync

An open-source platform to share Image, Audio and Video with devices synchronised to the millisecond. A 'Session Creator' to control the collective playback and final selection of content for a playlist. A simple idea, create shared playlists between people within proximity with all devices playing in concert.

However the idea quickly grew into a platform we hope to be used globally for Media Producer Broadcasting, Synchronised Meditations, Yoga and Sound Therapy.

The basic premise: Sound is the first sense we develop as infants and is the most powerful sense we have to elicit emotions. Using basic principles in physics such as harmonics, constructive wave formations, Binaural Beats (perceived sound differentials) and the fact that the interaction of individuals when combined produce a total effect that is greater than the sum of any individual contributor (synergism) we can create a stronger manifestation of these emotions with participants across the globe. Very Hippy, we know, but we have high hopes for this as a medium for Global Healing.

P2P Banking : Fee-Free Money

A new Transactions Platform based on a derivative of the Blockchain technology made infamous by Bitcoin, the first truly global shared ledger.

The API allows transactions to flow between parties within 1-2 seconds to facilitate POS transactions instead of longer clearance times with current Cryptocurrencies.

Not a Cryptocurrency. It can be used with any asset-class or crypto-contract, but a technology to facilitate transaction validation using Multi-party Cryptographic Protocols between 'trusted parties' instead of CPU work across the network.

Personal Banking. Any Asset

Designed for personal banking, POS transactions and investment. An ability to transact using any asset class - Currencies, Derivatives, Commodities, Debt Instruments, Micro-Investments, micro-loans...etc.
